About Sports Buddy

Sports Buddy is a platform designed to bring people together through the love of sports. Whether you're looking to organize events, or discover games nearby, Sports Buddy makes it easy to connect and play.

Our mission is to encourage an active lifestyle and create a community where everyone can participate in their favorite sports — regardless of experience or location.

From casual players to competitive athletes, we believe everyone deserves a buddy to play with. That’s why we built Sports Buddy — to make finding sports partners and events as easy as clicking a button.
